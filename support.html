Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sync my calendar across devices?
Liquid Calendar syncs automatically when you're signed in. Your events and preferences are stored securely in the cloud and available on all your devices.
Is my data private?
Yes. We take your privacy seriously. Please review our Privacy Policy for full details on how your data is handled.
How do I delete my account?
You can delete your account and all associated data from the app's Settings screen. If you need help, contact us at mrjoevu@gmail.com.
The app is crashing or not loading. What should I do?
Try force-quitting the app and reopening it. If the issue persists, make sure you're running the latest version from the App Store. Still stuck? Email us and we'll help.
Can I use Liquid Calendar for free?
Yes. Liquid Calendar is free to download and use. Some premium features may be available via optional in-app purchases.
