Privacy Policy

Liquid Calendar — Last updated: February 23, 2026

Liquid Calendar ("the App") is developed by Tanglefast. Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Calendar Events On-Device Synced

The App reads and writes events from your device's native calendar (via Apple Calendar / Google Calendar APIs) to display and manage your schedule. When you create an account and enable sync, your event data (title, date/time, location, notes) is stored in our cloud database to keep your devices in sync.

Voice Input On-Device

Voice-to-text uses Apple's on-device speech recognition. Audio is processed entirely on your device and is never sent to our servers or any third party.

Health Data On-Device

If you grant permission, the App reads sleep data from Apple HealthKit to adjust your morning schedule view. Health data stays on your device and is never transmitted, stored on our servers, or shared with anyone.

Account & Authentication Cloud

If you sign in (via Apple Sign-In), we store a minimal profile (user ID, email if provided) in our authentication system powered by Supabase to manage your account.

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — Authentication and cloud database for synced events. Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — Subscription and purchase management. Processes purchase receipts only. Privacy Policy
  • PostHog — Anonymous product analytics (disabled in development). Privacy Policy
  • Sentry — Crash reporting and performance monitoring (production only). Privacy Policy

3. AI & Natural Language Processing

The App uses natural language processing to parse text like "Lunch with Sarah on Friday at noon" into calendar events.

  • Currently, all text parsing happens on your device using built-in libraries. No event text is sent to external servers for processing.
  • In the future, the App may offer optional AI-powered features that send event text to third-party AI providers (such as Google Gemini or similar services) for enhanced natural language understanding. If introduced, this will require your explicit opt-in consent, and this policy will be updated before any such feature is enabled.
  • Your calendar data is never used to train AI models. Any future AI integrations will use API-only access, which by policy does not use customer data for model training (see Google Gemini API Terms).

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Cloud data is stored in Supabase (hosted on AWS) with row-level security policies ensuring you can only access your own data.
  • Authentication tokens are stored in your device's secure keychain (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore).
  • All network communication uses HTTPS/TLS encryption.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

  • You can delete individual events at any time from within the App.
  • To delete your account and all associated cloud data, contact us at the email below. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.
  • Crash reports and analytics data are retained for 90 days, then automatically purged.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for optional data processing
  • Export your data in a portable format

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the App or via the email associated with your account. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
